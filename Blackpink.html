Blackpink debuted on August 8th, 2016. BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The group debuted with their single album Square One, which featured "Whistle" and "Boombayah", their first number-one entries on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and the US Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, respectively. Referred to as the "biggest girl group in the world", Blackpink is the most successful Korean girl group internationally. BLACKPINK fans are called "BLINKS". Blackpink preformed in Coachella twice!

JISOO

Kim Jisoo also known as Jisoo is the unnie (oldest) of BLACKPINK. She was born on January 3rd 1995. She is currently 28 years old. She has 2 siblings. Every Blink is waiting for her solo to release. Her solo finally released on March 31, 2023. Jisoo is a South Korean singer and actress. She acted in a K-drama called "Snowdrop". In September 2022, Jisoo was awarded Outstanding Korean Actress at the Seoul International Drama Awards for her role in Snowdrop. In addition to her native language, Korean, Jisoo has also learned to speak Chinese, Japanese and English. In 2011, Jisoo joined YG Entertainment as a trainee after passing its auditions. Jisoo was ranked as the 10th most popular K-pop idol in 2018 and 17th in 2019. She appeared in promotions for Dior's new Bobby bag alongside other prominent influencers. Jisoo's favorite color is Purple.

JENNIE

Jennie Kim, known mononymously as Jennie, is a South Korean singer and rapper. Born and raised in South Korea, Jennie studied in New Zealand for five years before returning to South Korea in 2010. Jennie is the first member in Blackpink to release her solo. Her solo name was "SOLO". Upon release, "Solo" debuted on Gaon Music Chart at number one and achieved a triple crown for simultaneously topping the domestic digital, download, and streaming charts. The single remained the on the digital and mobile charts for two further consecutive weeks and on the streaming chart for a further three consecutive weeks. On June 1, 2016, Jennie was the first member to be revealed for YG Entertainment's newest girl group. Jennie appeared with Blackpink member Jisoo on Running Man in July 2018. Jennie's favorite colors are Black and Pink.

ROSÉ

Roseanne Park, known mononymously as Rosé, is a Korean-New Zealand singer and dancer based in South Korea. Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, Rosé signed with South Korean label YG Entertainment following a successful audition in 2012 and trained for four years before debuting as a member of the girl group Blackpink in August 2016. In March 2021, Rosé made her solo debut with her single album R. The album sold 448,089 copies in its first week, the highest for a Korean female soloist. It was the first song by a Korean solo artist to top the Billboard Global 200. She has 3 solos. On The Ground, Gone, and Hard To Love. She has an older sister. In 2004, at the age of seven, Rosé and her family moved to Melbourne, Australia. She began singing and learned to play guitar and piano as a child and performed in church choirs. Her favorite color is baby pink.

LISA

Lalisa Manobal (also spelled Manoban; born Pranpriya Manobal; March 27, 1997), known mononymously as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink formed by YG Entertainment. Lisa made her solo debut with her single album Lalisa in September 2021. She has 2 solos: Lalisa, and Money. Her favorite color is yellow. Lisa is YG's first non-korean artist. Lisa can speak various types of languages. She is Thai! Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer and model, currently based in South Korea. She is the youngest member of BLACKPINK. Lisa trained for more than five years before she joined Blackpink as the group's lead rapper and main dancer.
